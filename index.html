Welcome to Conor Homscheid's Portfolio

I am currently a senior at Washington University in St. Louis studying Systems Science and Engineering and double majoring in Finance. Originally from Connecticut, I stepped out of my comfort zone and decided to spend my 4 years of college more than 1,000 miles away from home. I have always enjoyed traveling ever since I was a little kid and never really found a passion for photography until I took a photography class in High School. Ever since then, a camera has always been at my hips whenever I travel to a new part of the world. I hope you enjoy some snapshots of my journies that I have taken over the past 6 years. Please feel free to send me an email regarding any of the locations that I have shot and please place an order for framed photographs as I will be donating the proceeds to an orphanage in Southern India. Each piece is $10 and you can find the order form below. Thanks and enjoy the snapshots of my travels!
